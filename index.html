FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What markets do you trade?
We focus exclusively on Index CFDs — specifically NAS100 (NASDAQ), SPX500 (S&P 500) and GER40 (DAX). This allows us to specialise and build deep expertise in these markets.
Is this suitable for beginners?
Yes. The educational videos cover the strategy from the ground up, and the daily outlooks and live calls give you real-world context. Whether you're new or experienced, the community is structured to meet you where you are.
How often are the live calls?
Twice per week. During each call, we review trades taken during the week, discuss expectations for the rest of the week and open the floor for Q&A.
Will this help me pass a prop firm challenge?
That's one of the key goals. The strategy, outlooks, recaps and community support are all designed to help you build the consistency and discipline needed to pass funding challenges.
What do the morning outlooks include?
Each morning, you get a detailed analysis of NAS100, SPX500 and DAX covering key levels, directional bias and the specific conditions needed to consider a trade. It's your pre-session preparation.
Am I going to get the attention I need?
Short answer: yes. This is intentionally kept as a small community — not a mass-membership platform. That means questions get answered, charts get reviewed and nobody gets lost in the noise. As a member, you're also locked in at your current price. Any future price increases won't affect you.
